About Ontrack Bike Rentals

Making bike rentals in Bangalore simple, affordable, and stress-free.

Who We Are

Ontrack Bike Rentals is a Bangalore-based two-wheeler rental company built with one clear mission — to remove the complexity, deposits, and limitations from traditional bike rentals.

We provide well-maintained bikes and scooters on flexible daily and monthly rental plans with zero security deposit, unlimited kilometers, and transparent pricing.

Quick Facts
  • ✔ Serving Bangalore riders
  • ✔ 35,000+ happy customers
  • ✔ Zero deposit rentals
  • ✔ Weekly & monthly plans
  • ✔ Doorstep delivery available

Our Story

Built by riders, for riders.

Ontrack was founded to solve everyday commuting challenges faced by students, professionals, freelancers, and delivery partners in Bangalore. Traditional rentals demanded heavy deposits, complex paperwork, and strict usage limits.

We changed that by introducing a seamless digital-first experience where customers can book online, verify instantly, and start riding within minutes — without worrying about kilometre caps or hidden costs.
